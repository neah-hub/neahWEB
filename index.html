About Me

Hello, and welcome.

This little corner of the internet was never intended to be a portfolio, nor a catalogue of accomplishments. It is simply a place where passing thoughts may linger a little longer, where beautiful things are gathered together, and where curiosity is allowed to wander without destination.

I have always been drawn to quiet places—libraries at dusk, handwritten letters, music carried by open windows, forgotten photographs, and stories that leave more questions than answers. This space is my attempt to preserve that feeling.

Perhaps every person deserves somewhere that belongs entirely to them, untouched by algorithms or endless timelines. For me, this is that place.
